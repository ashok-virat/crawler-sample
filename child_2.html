Jira Software

Jira Software is a powerful project management tool developed by Atlassian. It is designed to help software development teams plan, track, and manage their work efficiently. With features like agile boards, customizable workflows, and real-time reporting, Jira Software enables teams to collaborate effectively and deliver high-quality software.

Jira Core

Jira Core is a project management solution tailored for business teams. It provides tools for task tracking, project planning, and workflow management, allowing teams to organize and prioritize their work effectively. Jira Core integrates seamlessly with other Atlassian products, such as Confluence and Trello, to provide a comprehensive project management solution.

Jira Service Management

Jira Service Management (formerly known as Jira Service Desk) is a service management solution that helps IT and support teams deliver exceptional service experiences to their customers. It offers features like incident management, problem management, and change management, enabling teams to streamline their service delivery processes and improve customer satisfaction.
